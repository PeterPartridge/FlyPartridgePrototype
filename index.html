Consultancy Services

FlyPartridge LTD can assist with auditing the paperwork and administration processes of aircraft maintenance operations to facilitate meeting ESA Part 145 and CAME Part 21 standards. Our company is capable of overseeing the Base Maintenance certification using third party Part 145 approval for the types of aircraft listed below; Boeing 767-200 / 300 (CF6 80 Series engine) Type Approval (limitation 1). Boeing 737 CL (CFM 56 Series engine) Type Approval. Boeing 737 NG (CFM 56 Series engine) Type Approval. Boeing 757 RB211 Type Approval (Limitation 1)

Aircraft Maintenance Services

We can provide aircraft maintenance service on a variety of aircraft; Boeing 767-200 / 300 (CF6 80 Series engine) Type Approval (limitation 1). Boeing 737 CL (CFM 56 Series engine) Type Approval. Boeing 737 NG (CFM 56 Series engine) Type Approval. With the variety of services offered being; Troubleshooting, defect rectification and Service (A) checks in a Line Maintenance Environment. Minor checks, component changes, defect troubleshooting, rectification and special inspections and Borescope inspection. C Check’s including 8 C’s (D check). Incorporation of AD’s, SB’s, inspection defect rectification and structural repairs e.g. floor beams, skin repairs, frames and stringers, pylon mods etc . Scope of work ranges from under carriage changes, flight control surface and actuator changes. Engine changes and all other Major and minor component changes e.g. hyd pumps fuel pumps, HMG’s. Jacking and control surface balancing.

Our staff hold fully CAA issued Part 66 B1 licences while being self-motivated and self-starters and are able to work as part of a team or as an individual. Our staff can reliably set and achieve targets regularly within Line, Minor and Base maintenance programmes. Our employees are able man managers, with experience at prestigious companies including Minor / Line work working with TUIFly Nordic, Tech4Jets, SRT, Storm Aviation and Base Maintenance with MNG and Shannon Aerospace.
